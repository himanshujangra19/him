Green foods often refer to vegetables that are green in color, such as spinach, broccoli, kale, lettuce, and peas. These vegetables are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, and they are a crucial part of a balanced and healthy diet. They provide nutrients like vitamin K, vitamin A, fiber, and folate.Some people use the term "green food" to refer to "green superfoods." These are nutrient-dense foods that are often marketed for their potential health benefits. Examples include spirulina, chlorella, wheatgrass, and barley grass.
